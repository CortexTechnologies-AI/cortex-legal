Cortex Technologies ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

We use the information we collect to:

If you opt in to receive SMS notifications from Cortex Technologies:
